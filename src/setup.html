Setup Your Spotify App

To use Spotify Native Toggler, create your Spotify app and provide your credentials for seamless integration.

Important: Only Spotify Premium users can control playback (play, pause, skip, volume) with this app. Do not share your Client ID or Client Secret with anyone.
1 Create a Spotify App
Visit the Spotify Developer Dashboard and follow these steps:
  • Click "Create App"
  • Enter an app name (e.g., "My Spotify Controller")
  • Add this redirect URI:
http://127.0.0.1:3000/callback
  • Select "Web API" in the APIs section
  • Agree to terms and create the app
2 Get Your Credentials
In your new Spotify app:
  • Copy the Client ID
  • Click "Show client secret" and copy the Client Secret
